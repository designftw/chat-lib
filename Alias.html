Class: Alias

Alias(id, createdAt, updatedAt, name, payload)

The alias is the recipient or sender of messages. One account can have multiple aliases, for example an account could have a personal and work alias. The chat UI could allow the user to send and receive messages from multiple aliases at once. Similar to associating multiple email addresses with on gmail account, or having multiple slack workspaces logged in.
